About Peninsula

Peninsula is a surf school based in Las Terrenas, Samaná — focused on quality lessons at Playa Bonita. No camp, no fluff. Just surf coaching that helps you progress fast and feel safe in the water.

Meet your instructor

Cristian Padilla teaches with a calm, clear approach: ocean awareness first, technique second, confidence always. Whether it’s your first time standing up or you’re refining turns and timing, Cristian keeps it structured and fun.

Where we teach

  • Main spot: Playa Bonita, Las Terrenas (Samaná)
  • Also available: Cabarete (by request)
  • Lesson style: private or small group
  • Focus: safety, fundamentals, fast progression
